Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Lumicello

What age range is LumiBox designed for?

Our First Year Collection is designed for babies 0-12 months, with six age-specific kits tailored to each developmental stage. We're working on expanding to toddler and preschool age ranges soon!

How do Curio Types work?

Curio Types is our unique system that maps your child's interests, learning styles, and engagement patterns into a multidimensional profile. It evolves over time to provide increasingly personalized recommendations.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within Thailand. We're planning to expand to other countries soon, starting with Southeast Asian neighbors. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when we launch in your area!

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Yes! You can cancel or modify your order within 24 hours of placing it. After that, please contact us as soon as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

Is the digital platform available yet?

We're currently in development and will be launching soon. Follow us on Instagram to get early access, connect with other parents, and be the first to know when we launch!
